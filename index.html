Meet

Preetika Singh

(Brand Technical Head)

Preetika Singh is the Brand Technical Head and Nail Educator at Ellement Co. Nail Academy. With over 7 years of experience in the nail industry, she has trained and mentored countless aspiring nail artists, helping them grow from curious beginners into confident professionals.

Acrylics and miniature nail art are her biggest passions, she finds something truly magical about creating intricate, detailed designs on such a small canvas. For Preetika, great nail art lives in the details, and she's passionate about helping students develop that same precision and eye for craft.

Driven by a deep love for the industry, she's dedicated to inspiring, guiding, and helping every student achieve their best, because passion deserves the right mentorship to become a real career.
